[1:00 - ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let us walk through the architecture of the system.
First, user inputs go through the Security Checkpoint to sanitize parameter boundaries and prevent raw text exports in telemetry.
Next, requests hit SprintPilot AI's RootAgent, which orchestrates six business operations tools.
Depending on the goal, the RootAgent can trigger generate_business_plan to output market strategies, generate_project_requirements to compile product specs, or generate_user_stories to draft Agile user cases.
It also uses create_project_roadmap to phase deliverables, generate_documentation to document codebase elements, and analyze_business_risks to evaluate industry compliance.
Finally, execution traces are securely sent to Cloud Logging via OpenTelemetry collectors.
