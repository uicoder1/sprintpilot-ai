[1:10 - THE SOLUTION]

"To solve this, we created SprintPilot AI—an Autonomous AI Business Operations Assistant. Unlike traditional, chatty bots that pause at every single step to ask you what to do next, SprintPilot AI operates as a fully autonomous workflow orchestrator.

[2:00 - ARCHITECTURE WALKTHROUGH]
Visual: Show assets/architecture_diagram.png.
Audio:
"Let's look at the underlying architecture that makes this possible. 

SprintPilot AI is built natively using the Google Agent Development Kit (ADK) 2.0. When an idea is received by our FastAPI backend, it routes into our RootAgent orchestrator. 

Rather than executing ad-hoc steps, the orchestrator triggers the `execute_business_planning_workflow` node. This launches a sequential pipeline of specialized agent tools: from business plan creation to functional PRD extraction, story building, roadmap scheduling, risk evaluation, and documentation compile. The results are gathered and delivered as a complete, unified markdown report. 

Furthermore, the RootAgent is connected to Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers, allowing it to autonomously read/write local filesystem files, log GitHub issues, upload to Google Drive, draft Google Docs, and schedule calendar events directly from the generated project timeline."
